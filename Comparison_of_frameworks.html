Comparison of Security Frameworks

A framework, in the context of cyber security, is a thought process for cyber security.  My observation is that some frameworks are very concise and rather vague (CIA) while some are really quite specific (The top 18 or top 20 controls).

This comparison considers each framework in terms of specificity, applicability, and detail.  These metrics are subjective, base on my reading.
