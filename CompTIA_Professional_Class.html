How to take great screenshots

  1. Echo your name and date in the output.
  2. Use a third-party software like Greenshot.
  3. Select only the pertinent area of your screen.
  4. Save the images as a jpg or png.
  5. Name them: [Year-Month-Day_Hour:Minute_Lastname_Firstname_Labname_01, 02, 03, 04...]

The Challenge question:

In a professional IT security position, are there any duties or responsibilities that you would not assume? What about others? What might their “blind spots” be?  For example, it is not difficult to imagine a perfectly competent IT security pro that will never investigate their supervisors or chain of command even if there is clear evidence of wrongdoing. Why? Perhaps out of a deep sense of loyalty to their country and mission.  What about other motivations like job insecurity, extortion, espionage, blackmail, reputation loss, trust issues, or other reasons?
