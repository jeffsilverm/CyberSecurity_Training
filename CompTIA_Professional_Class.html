How to take great screenshots

  1. Echo your name and date in the output.
  2. Use a third-party software like Greenshot.
  3. Select only the pertinent area of your screen.
  4. Save the images as a jpg or png.
  5. Name them: [Year-Month-Day_Hour:Minute_Lastname_Firstname_Labname_01, 02, 03, 04...]
